General Rules

Rule 1: Respect & Etiquette

Maintaining respectful behavior is essential to creating a positive and welcoming environment for all members. This means treating everyone with kindness, consideration, and empathy. Every member should feel safe and valued, regardless of their background, opinions, or identity.

No Death Threats

Death threats are completely unacceptable. Threatening another member with harm or implying that they should be harmed is not only disrespectful but also illegal. This includes:

  • Direct threats
  • Indirect suggestions
  • Language that makes others feel unsafe

❌ Example: "I hope you die" or "Someone should get rid of you"

No Sexism

Sexism, or discrimination based on gender, is not tolerated. All members, regardless of their gender identity, should be treated equally. This includes avoiding:

  • Sexist jokes
  • Discriminatory comments
  • Gender stereotypes

❌ Example: "Women are bad at gaming" or "Men don't understand emotions"

No Hate Speech or Racism

Hate speech and racism are strictly forbidden. This includes any language, symbols, or actions that promote discrimination based on:

  • Race or ethnicity
  • Religion
  • Nationality

This includes coded language and discriminatory memes.

No Doxxing, Swatting, or Witch Hunting

The following actions are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate action:

  • Sharing personal information without consent (doxxing)
  • Making false emergency calls (swatting)
  • Organizing harassment campaigns (witch hunting)

Promoting Respectful Communication

Focus on positive interactions and constructive feedback. When disagreeing:

✅ Good: "I see your point, but I think differently because..."

❌ Bad: "You're wrong and stupid"

Rule 2: No Advertising

Advertising is strictly prohibited in our community to maintain a focus on meaningful interactions and discussions. This rule helps prevent clutter and ensures that members are not bombarded with unsolicited promotions.

Prohibition of Advertising in Public Channels

Any form of advertising in public channels is not allowed. This includes:

  • Business promotions
  • YouTube channels
  • Twitch streams
  • Social media accounts
  • Other Discord servers

❌ Example: "Check out my new YouTube channel at youtube.com/..."

No Advertising via Direct Messages

Sending unsolicited advertisements via direct messages (DMs) is prohibited. Members should not receive promotional messages without their consent.

  • No unsolicited store links
  • No server invites
  • No promotional content

❌ Example: Sending DMs with "Join my new Discord server!"

Exceptions for Designated Areas

If the server has a designated channel for sharing personal projects or advertisements, you may post there according to the specific guidelines of that channel. However:

  • Posts must follow community standards
  • No spam or excessive posting
  • Follow channel-specific guidelines

✅ Good: Posting in #self-promo channel following guidelines

No Affiliate Links or Referral Codes

Sharing affiliate links or referral codes is not permitted. This includes:

  • Commission-based links
  • Referral programs
  • Affiliate marketing content

❌ Example: "Use my code DISCORD20 for 20% off"

Respecting the Community's Purpose

Our community is built on shared interests and respectful interactions. Remember:

  • Focus on genuine conversations
  • Contribute meaningful content
  • Avoid commercial exploitation

✅ Good: Engaging in discussions about shared interests

❌ Bad: Using discussions to promote products

Rule 3: No NSFW Content

To ensure a safe and inclusive environment for all members, the sharing of NSFW (Not Safe For Work) content is strictly prohibited. This includes any material that is explicit, inappropriate, or potentially harmful.

No Gore

Graphic content that depicts violence, injury, or extreme harm is not allowed. This includes:

  • Violent images or videos
  • Accident footage
  • Injury depictions
  • Descriptions of physical harm

❌ Example: Sharing images of accidents or injuries

No Sexual Content

Content that is sexually explicit or suggestive is forbidden:

  • Pornographic material
  • Explicit images or videos
  • Sexual descriptions
  • Suggestive content

❌ Example: Posting links to adult content or explicit imagery

Keep Content Safe for All Users

All shared content must be appropriate for a diverse audience:

  • Family-friendly material only
  • Age-appropriate content
  • No questionable websites
  • Clean memes and jokes

✅ Good: Sharing fun, clean memes everyone can enjoy

❌ Bad: Posting content with adult themes or innuendos

Profile Guidelines

Profile pictures and banners must be appropriate:

  • No explicit imagery
  • No violent content
  • No suggestive material
  • Family-friendly avatars only

❌ Example: Using inappropriate or suggestive profile pictures

Reporting Procedures

When encountering NSFW content:

  • Report to moderators immediately
  • Do not engage with the content
  • Avoid sharing or responding
  • Wait for moderator action

✅ Good: "I've reported this content to the moderators."

❌ Bad: Engaging with or sharing the inappropriate content

Rule 4: No Spamming

Spamming disrupts the flow of conversation and can be highly irritating to other members. To ensure that our community remains enjoyable and respectful, spamming in any form is strictly prohibited.

Message Spam

Sending multiple messages in quick succession or repetitive content is prohibited:

  • No repeated messages
  • No flooding chat with random text
  • No excessive emoji spam
  • No meaningless content

❌ Example: Sending "Hi" multiple times or "asdfasdf" repeatedly

Voice Channel Disruptions

Voice channels should remain clear of disruptive sounds:

  • No soundboard spam
  • No disruptive voice changers
  • No ear-splitting sounds
  • No sudden loud noises

❌ Example: Playing loud sirens or using extreme voice modifications

Channel Etiquette

Respect channel purposes and topics:

  • Stay on topic in dedicated channels
  • No off-topic discussions
  • Follow channel-specific guidelines
  • Use appropriate channels for content

✅ Good: Discussing tech support in #tech-help

❌ Bad: Gaming discussions in #homework-help

Bot Usage and Tagging

Responsible use of bots and mentions:

  • No excessive bot commands
  • Use designated channels for bot commands
  • No unnecessary @mentions
  • Avoid @everyone/@here abuse

❌ Example: Repeatedly using music bot commands or unnecessary @everyone pings

Reporting Spam

When encountering spam:

  • Report to moderators
  • Don't engage with spammers
  • Wait for moderator action
  • Use proper reporting channels

✅ Good: "I've reported the spam to moderators."

❌ Bad: Engaging with or encouraging the spammer

Rule 5: Avoid Sensitive Topics

To maintain harmony and prevent unnecessary conflicts, discussions on sensitive topics are discouraged in our community. This rule helps ensure that the server remains a welcoming and safe space for everyone.

No Political Debates

Political discussions can quickly become heated and divisive. To avoid conflicts and maintain a positive environment:

  • No election discussions
  • No political party debates
  • Avoid governmental policy discussions
  • Keep political opinions private

❌ Example: Starting debates about elections or political parties

Avoid Religious Discussions

Religion is a deeply personal and sensitive subject that requires careful consideration:

  • No religious debates
  • Avoid proselytizing
  • Respect all beliefs
  • Keep religious views private

❌ Example: Attempting to convert others or debating religious doctrines

No Controversial Social Issues

To maintain a peaceful environment, avoid discussing controversial social topics:

  • Gender identity debates
  • Sexuality discussions
  • Social justice arguments
  • Controversial current events

❌ Example: Starting debates about social movements or identity politics

Maintaining Positive Discussion

Focus on building a supportive community through:

  • Shared interests and hobbies
  • Positive interactions
  • Inclusive conversations
  • Constructive discussions

✅ Good: Discussing shared hobbies and interests

❌ Bad: Engaging in polarizing debates

Reporting Guidelines

When sensitive topics arise:

  • Report to moderators
  • Avoid escalating the situation
  • Disengage from heated discussions
  • Wait for moderator intervention

✅ Good: "Let's keep the conversation friendly and inclusive"

❌ Bad: Engaging in or encouraging controversial debates

Rule 6: No Malicious Content

The sharing of malicious content is strictly prohibited to maintain the safety and integrity of our community. This rule encompasses a wide range of harmful activities and materials.

No Sharing of Harmful Links

Sharing malicious links is strictly forbidden. This includes:

  • Phishing sites
  • Malware links
  • Virus downloads
  • Deceptive URLs

❌ Example: Posting links claiming to be free downloads that actually contain malware

No Distribution of Viruses or Trojans

Sharing harmful software is prohibited:

  • Viruses and malware
  • Trojans and backdoors
  • Malicious scripts
  • Harmful executables

❌ Example: Sharing files disguised as games that contain viruses

No Crash Videos or Harmful Media

Content designed to disrupt or crash devices is forbidden:

  • Browser crash videos
  • System-freezing content
  • Malicious scripts
  • Harmful media files

❌ Example: Posting videos with hidden scripts that crash browsers

Avoiding Phishing Attempts

Deceptive practices to obtain personal information are prohibited:

  • Impersonation attempts
  • Credential harvesting
  • Social engineering
  • False login pages

❌ Example: Pretending to be staff and requesting login information

Reporting Procedures

When encountering suspicious content:

  • Report to moderators immediately
  • Do not interact with suspicious content
  • Avoid clicking unknown links
  • Wait for moderator action

✅ Good: "I've reported the suspicious link to moderators"

❌ Bad: Clicking or sharing suspicious content

Rule 7: No Self Bots

The use of self-bots, including Nitro snipers or bots like Nighty, is strictly prohibited in our community. This rule is in place to maintain fairness, security, and a positive user experience for all members.

Definition of Self-Bots

Self-bots are accounts running automated scripts or bots that perform actions on behalf of a user:

  • Automated message sending
  • Automated reactions
  • Automated tasks and scripts
  • Unauthorized automation tools

❌ Example: Using scripts to automatically respond to messages

Examples of Prohibited Self-Bots

The following types of self-bots are not allowed:

  • Nitro Snipers
  • Spam Bots
  • Auto Moderation Bots
  • Other Automated Tools

❌ Example: Using Nitro sniper bots to automatically claim gift codes

Reasons for Prohibition

Self-bots are prohibited for several important reasons:

  • Fairness: Prevents unfair advantages
  • Security: Protects account integrity
  • User Experience: Maintains genuine interactions
  • TOS Compliance: Follows Discord's rules

✅ Good: Engaging naturally with other members

❌ Bad: Using automated tools for interactions

Consequences of Using Self-Bots

Violations will result in immediate action:

  • Warnings for minor infractions
  • Temporary bans for repeated offenses
  • Permanent bans for severe violations
  • Account reporting to Discord

❌ Example: Using automation tools resulting in permanent ban

Reporting Guidelines

When suspecting self-bot usage:

  • Report to moderators promptly
  • Provide detailed evidence
  • Document suspicious behavior
  • Await moderator investigation

✅ Good: "I've noticed suspicious automated behavior from this user"

❌ Bad: Taking matters into your own hands

Rule 8: Avoid DMing Staff

To maintain efficient communication and a positive atmosphere within our community, we encourage members to refrain from sending direct messages (DMs) to the staff team unless absolutely necessary.

Reasons for Restriction

Understanding why this rule exists helps maintain effective community management:

  • Channel Use: Use designated channels for inquiries
  • Respecting Staff Boundaries
  • Time Efficiency
  • Community Transparency

✅ Good: Using appropriate channels for general questions

When DMs are Appropriate

Direct messages to staff are acceptable in specific situations:

  • Sensitive personal issues
  • Private concerns
  • Urgent server matters
  • Confidential reports

✅ Example: Reporting sensitive content privately

❌ Example: Asking about server rules that are publicly available

Communication Guidelines

When DMing staff is necessary, follow these guidelines:

  • Be clear and concise
  • Provide relevant details
  • Exercise patience
  • Respect staff time

✅ Good: "Hi, I'm reporting [specific issue] with evidence attached"

❌ Bad: "Hey" or multiple messages without context

Staff Availability

Remember these important points about staff availability:

  • No spam messages
  • Use moderation channels
  • Allow response time
  • Respect off-hours

❌ Example: Sending multiple follow-up messages when staff hasn't responded

Consequences of Misuse

Inappropriate DM behavior may result in:

  • Warnings from staff
  • Temporary restrictions
  • DM privileges revocation
  • Account sanctions

❌ Example: Spamming staff DMs with non-urgent matters

✅ Good: Following proper channels for communication

Moderation Guidelines

Wick Bot Features and Capabilities

Our server utilizes Wick Premium for automated moderation and security. Here are the key features and capabilities:

Automated Monitoring and Rule Enforcement

Wick provides constant monitoring of:

  • Chat messages and user actions
  • Spam detection
  • Offensive language
  • Harassment prevention
  • Inappropriate media filtering

Points-Based Warning System

Infractions result in points that determine consequences:

  • 10 Points: Timeout for 10 minutes
  • 20 Points: Timeout for 30 minutes
  • 30 Points: Timeout for 1 hour
  • 40 Points: Timeout for 8 hours
  • 50 Points: Permanent Ban

Comprehensive Logging and Tracking

Wick Premium logs all server activity including:

  • Message content (sent, edited, deleted)
  • Channel activity
  • Profile changes
  • Join and leave dates
  • Behavioral patterns

Suspicious Account Detection

Wick monitors for suspicious behavior such as:

  • Unusual frequency of joins and leaves
  • Use of high-risk language or links
  • Frequent profile changes
  • Rapid posting in multiple channels

Enhanced Security Protocols

Advanced security features include:

  • Anti-raid protection
  • Anti-spam mechanisms
  • Dangerous link filtering
  • Media content screening

Appeals Process

Members can appeal infractions through:

  • Contacting moderators for review
  • Providing context for the situation
  • Reviewing logged evidence
  • Following up on appeal decisions

Key Information for Members

Guidelines and Monitoring

  • Follow community guidelines carefully
  • All actions are monitored and logged
  • Infractions accumulate points over time
  • Suspicious behavior triggers additional scrutiny

Account Security

  • Activities are tracked for community safety
  • Message edits and deletions are logged
  • Profile changes are monitored
  • Join/leave patterns are analyzed

Appeals and Support

  • Unfair infractions can be appealed
  • Complete logs available for review
  • Moderators assess appeal evidence
  • Decisions based on documented behavior
